(Downloading Valorant and Launching it) 1.Head to the official Valorant website and click on the Play Now or the Play Free button. 2.Your web browser will now begin downloading the Installer package. 3.Click on the downloaded file and choose your preferred download location. You require 8 GB of free space to install the game. 4.You need to sign into a Riot Games account to login and start playing once your download completes. 5.After the download, a new icon should show up on your Desktop to launch Valorant. You can also search for “Valorant” in your Windows search bar and launch the game. (Creating a Riot Games ID) Go to Riot Games’ official website. 1.Click on 'Create Account', and you will be redirected to the sign-up page. 2.Enter your email address and click on the Start button to begin registration. 3.Fill in the required details including date of birth, username, and password. 4,Confirm your registration.
